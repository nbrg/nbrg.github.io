NBRG head out West

10 Sep, 2015

To wrap up and put a bow on the 2015 Derby year for Northern Beaches Roller Girls they are playing in a fun invitational bout against Western Sydney Rollers. The Sirens will be playing in the opening game for WSRs Grand Final event.

Find out more about the whole day on the Facebook event.

Our game will start 28 Nov, 2015 at 3:00pm.
